Gameplay

Clash Royale gameplay

Players use decks of 8 cards to destroy enemy towers. Matches last 3 minutes (or longer in overtime). The classic match - 1v1 battle you need to destroy enemy towers and earn trophies to unlock arenas, cards, and ranked leagues. Matches take place in an arena with King and Princess Towers. You spend elixir to deploy cards. The game also features modes like 2v2, challenges, Clan Wars, and rotating limited-time events.

Main gameplay points:

  • Elixir management
  • Card placement
  • Great timing, prediction and strategy
