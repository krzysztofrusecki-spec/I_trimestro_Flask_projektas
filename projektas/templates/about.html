About Clash Royale

Key information

Clash Royale is a real-time strategy free to play mobile game. It's a multiplayer game where you collect and upgrade Clash characters, battle opponents, destroy their towers, win Trophies, Crowns, and glory in the Arena.

  • Developer: Supercell
  • Genre: Strategy, PvP
